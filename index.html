About

Maze ballz is a game created by a anonymous person. In this game you're a human who has been turned into a ball by a mysterious being and your objective is to collect the rotating cubes. The rotating cubes are charges of energy once you collect all of the rotating cubes your character has enough energy to hyperdrive to the next area.

If you want to play Mazeball there is a zip file below. To play the game you have to download the zip file and extract the zip file to the desktop
